About The Project

The project was initiated in the month of August, 2019 with a handful of plants in small pots. With the development we made a green house increased the number of plants. Now with 15 plants we have been working upon to make people aware about these plants with their medicinal importance. With the help of this project we are trying to help people know how the plant looks like and locate the plant within the university campus. We hope to make health of the people better with the help of herbs so that the side effects due to medicines can be reduce and we can look towards a better, healthier and happier world.
